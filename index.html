About Me

I'm an AI and Robotics Engineer with experience in both research and industry. My passion lies in transforming complex AI concepts into deployable solutions—whether it's enabling drones to autonomously navigate tomato fields in southern Italy, or training end-to-end pipelines for information extraction from unstructured documents.

I've worked on real-world systems involving UAVs, robotic manipulators (UR10), and advanced perception tasks using Python, C++, ROS, and deep learning frameworks. During my time as a Research Fellow at CNR and an AI Engineer at Botshelf.ai, I've contributed to open-field drone missions, developed path planning strategies, and optimized document intelligence systems by fusing OCR, layout models, and LLMs.

I'm driven by the challenge of taking an idea from a paper and turning it into a system that works in the wild. Whether it’s 3D grasping, coreference resolution, or object recognition, I enjoy working at the intersection of AI, robotics, and applied problem-solving.
