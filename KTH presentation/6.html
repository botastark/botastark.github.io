Multi-Horizon Planner Architecture

PLANNER EXTENSION: MCTS Variants • Reward Decomposition • Coordination

AGRITECH

Hierarchical Levels

HLP (High-Level) Strategic
  • Method: UCB Tree Search (Full MCTS)
  • Horizon: 10 Regions (Long-term)
  • Compute: ~30 Iterations
  • Reuses tree across steps
LLP (Low-Level) Reactive
  • Method: Random Rollout Sampling
  • Horizon: 3 Steps (Short-term)
  • Compute: ~50 Iterations
  • Fast, no tree storage

Reward Function

g = g1 + g2

g1:

Discounted IG (Immediate Information Gain). Drives local exploration.

g2:

Mission Time Estimate. Encodes global progress & completion speed.

Vertical Flow

Top-Down (Guidance)

HLP guides via g2 conditioning. LLP optimizes local moves that align with global time reduction.

Bottom-Up (Feedback)

LLP sends true IG & uncertainty updates. Triggers HLP to re-evaluate region priorities.

Intent Structures

HIGH LEVEL INTENT

Sequence of Regions + ETA.

e.g., [Region 3, Region 7...]

LOW LEVEL INTENT

Sequence of Actions + Footprints.

e.g., [Forward, Right, Up...]

Swarm Sharing

  • Intent Broadcasting

    Agents share intent summaries, not full plans. Low bandwidth.

  • Coverage Masks

    Teammate intents create "discount masks" to prevent redundant coverage.
