Key Results

Adaptive vs. Lawnmower

IPP enables more efficient coverage and higher mapping accuracy than traditional lawnmower patterns by focusing on high-uncertainty areas.

Sampling Efficiency

Minimal upfront sampling (single image per altitude) suffices to calibrate the sensor model for specific field conditions.

Orthomap Validation

Tested on real wheat fields (Adria, Italy). Adaptive pairwise weighting yielded the most stable coverage despite real-world noise.
